About Us

Vardaan Electronics Industries Pvt. Ltd., is an ISO 9001 certified company established in 2021. We specialize in the manufacturing of IPIS, PA, PIS, infotainment systems, and CCTV systems for Rolling Stock applications.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of integrated electronic systems for railways. We strive for innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction by delivering cutting-edge technology for efficient operations and seamless passenger experiences.

Our Expertise
  • PA Systems: Clear real-time announcements across stations and platforms.
  • PIS: Deliver timely information for passenger convenience and transparency.
  • IPIS Displays: Show real-time train schedules, arrivals, and announcements.
  • CCTV Solutions: High-definition security for stations and on-board environments.
